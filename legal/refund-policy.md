## 1. The core principle

Graba is a booking intermediary. When you book a flight, hotel, or transfer, you're bound by **that supplier's own fare rules and cancellation policy**, not a Graba-specific one — we cannot offer a refund on a booking that the underlying airline or hotel has marked non-refundable, because we do not control that inventory.
